## Reporting a Vulnerability

Please report security vulnerabilities by emailing vishal.vasistha1@gmail.com. Do not open a public issue.

Include:
- Description of the vulnerability
- Steps to reproduce
- Potential impact
- Suggested fix (if any)

We aim to respond within 72 hours and will work with you to verify and address the issue.
